And the next great wave of violence is what we were talking about a minute ago with collectivization, which claims the lives of millions of people. And then there is what's known as the Great Purge, which is a discrete political event and it has a kind of fever curve of delirious political radicalism. This is the sort of thing that you will have seen films about where, you know, people are in their apartment at night in Moscow. Often it's quite a modern apartment with a telephone.
